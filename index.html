Dreaming about a live show of your favourite band in your town? We definitely can help! RASSEYE! provides free and convenient tools for planning and organizing shows to everyone. Want to see your idol on stage next door? We'll help you collect votes from your friends and neighbours, analyze all the stats and publish results so that your star can hear you!

All you are supposed to do is to give us the details about your desired show: the star's name, your location and preferred concert date. Right after you submit the information, the voting starts. Share the voting link with people from your town and track the insreasing interest in the show! The goal is to collect as many votes as possible in a fixed period of time. If the number of votes is sufficiently large, we will publish the stats to let the world know about the future concert. We are here to help you collect votes and to advertise your desired concert as well.
